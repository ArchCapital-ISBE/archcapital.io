Our Projects

Accel Resumes

Accel Resumes is currently Arch’s largest project, and it was developed completely in house! Accel is a resume writing, reviewing, and critiquing service that costs pennies on the dollar compared to its competitors - and for good reason. Accel targets those who haven’t yet entered the workforce but nowadays need more help than ever with their resumes: college students. That’s the main market, but not the only one! Within the coming months, we’ll be launching a huge marketing campaign to get Accel’s name out there to thousands of people across the country, and we can’t wait!

2U

2U is another proprietary business one hundred percent developed by Arch members. It aims to essentially be postmates on campus, but cheaper, more efficient, and all-around better. 2U looks to serve NU students looking for that late night snack while studying - we’ll be delivering food and drink from vendors located on Northwestern’s campus directly 2U! Keep your eyes out for it coming this Spring quarter!

Spark

Spark is Arch’s Facebook presence and aims to be Northwestern’s main resource for entrepreneurship. It contains not only entrepreneurship related articles, but also a forum that allows people to make connections that can help build a business, foster creative thinking, or find out about anything entrepreneurship related that’s happening at Northwestern!

Boom

Boom is a social media app out of UC - Santa Barbara that we’re inclined to believe will be the next big thing. Boom essentially is a location-based events app that allows people to anonymously (or not) share photos and videos to a feed that answers the age-old question of, “What’s going on tonight?” In this app you’ll see all kinds of events happening on and around campus - everything from parties to student group meetings to concerts!

Ussie

In today’s world of unrelenting pressures coming from social media, Ussie looks to be the social network that won’t have you preoccupied with getting likes or any sort of approval. Ussie is a group-centered app that allows groups of friends and family to directly share photos and other media with each other in a private, more comfortable manner.
